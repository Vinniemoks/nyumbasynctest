Key Features

Everything you need to manage your properties efficiently

Online Rent Collection

Accept payments via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or credit card. Automated reminders and receipts included.

Maintenance Tracking

Tenants can submit requests, and managers can track progress from submission to completion.

Financial Reporting

Generate detailed reports on rental income, expenses, and occupancy rates at any time.
